Pesto Pasta

Description

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner. This versatile pesto pasta recipe is easy to customize by adding your favorite protein, if you like. Chicken, sausage, and chickpeas are all delicious mix-ins.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion and oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasoning
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Cheese

Step-By-Step

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasoning.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pas and toss with cheese.
